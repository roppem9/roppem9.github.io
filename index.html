Get to Know Me

I am an Interactive Designer living in the great state of Tennessee. When not at work, you can find me freelancing, listening to the next great podcast (I’m currently obsessed with Embedded and Start Up Podcast), or traveling to someplace warm. I moved to Chattanooga two and a half years ago with a freshly minted degree from Wake Forest University and a fifteen-year-old borrowed suburban full of all my life’s possessions. I’m an advocate for women in design & technology and am involved in local organizations such as Code XX, a women’s coding group, and Cha Devs, a weekly luncheon for Chattanooga Devs. I’ve been a member of AIGA since 2012 and sit on the board of directors locally.
