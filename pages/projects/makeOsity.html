Identity Work for NYC Start-Up

MakeOsity, Inc.

Project Overview

I was outsourced by this early stage, NYC-based startup for brand development and production. My client wanted to concentrate on the MakeOsity “M” with creative reminiscent of a super hero's emblem.

Process

My main goal with this project was to capture the brand’s entrepreneurial spirit through strong visuals that would appeal to a younger demographic. All artwork for this project was created using Illustrator and Photoshop.
