UI Design & Consulting

Smart Furniture

Project Overview

I was tasked with creating a more intuitive, streamlined product page and shopping cart, one that fit the site's pre-existing, mobile first Bootstrap 3 framework and incorporated new site additions such as less ambiguous shipping lead times and a restructured icon system.

Other Projects: I also worked on designing and building furniture buying guides, a post-purchase shipment tracker, modal pop-ups, upgrades to mobile navigation, and better organized in-stock pop-ups.
